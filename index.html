How to Use Go Picasso!

Color Picker

Drag the circle and use the color scale to pick various colors for your painting.

Paint Stroke Scale

Slide the scale to customize the size of your paint strokes. Paint your lines thin or wide, you decide!

Shape Button

Click to add a shape to your artwork and alter it's color and size.
