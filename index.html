How to Use Go Picasso!

Color Picker

Drag the circle and use the color scale to pick various colors for your painting.

Paint Stroke Scale

Slide the scale to customize the size of your paint strokes.
Paint your lines thin or wide, you decide!

Toggle Button

Use the toggle button to swtich between light and dark themes to customize the look of your canvas.
